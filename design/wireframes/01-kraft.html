WHY WE ESCALATED

decision rationale

The agent couldn't confidently match Patterson's part numbers to our catalog. Every one of the 22 lines used a customer-side code that doesn't exist in our item master — their SKUs are formatted for their ERP, not ours.

Guessing = wrong stock, wrong invoice, angry plant manager.

So the agent did the opposite of guess. It froze the write, opened a single exception for a human to glance at, and drafted a clarification email asking the customer to share their catalog mapping or send the order in our SKU format.

Zero rows entered the sales-orders collection. One row entered the exceptions collection. A human gets a ping, opens the dashboard, sees everything needed to respond, and ships a decision in under a minute. That's the win.
